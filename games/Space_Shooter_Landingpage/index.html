Space Shooter

Player's ship YT-1930 Skywalker runs in space, meet lines of enemies on his way and fights a battle. The player wins when all enemies will be down. Be carefull, on your way you can meet asteroids.

How to handle

On the Top Panel you can restart game, see your score and how many rockets stay, pause or continue game. Max count rockets is 50.

To handle player you can use buttons on left control bar or hot keys:

To move up use ↑ or W

To move down ↓ or X

To rotate counterclockwise use ← or A

To rotate clockwise use → or D

To make player stop or run again use S

For fire use Space or Z
